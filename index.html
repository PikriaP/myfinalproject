About

WOMEN IN TECH
is a non-profit organisation
which helps women to learn IT courses for free

our organisation

focuses on empowering women in tech and redefining the industry so that women are equally represented at leaders, executives, founders, board members and software engineers. The focus is on empowering women with the coding and programming skills they need to advance in their careers, educating companies on how to promote, retain and hire women and establishing a global community of mentorship and support for women engineers.
